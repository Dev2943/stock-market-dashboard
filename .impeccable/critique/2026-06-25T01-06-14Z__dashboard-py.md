# Critique: dashboard.py — Senior UX Review (Bloomberg/TradingView Lens)

Two independent assessments (LLM design review + deterministic code/detector audit) were synthesized and cross-verified against the live source.

## Heuristics (from Assessment A)
Total: 19/40 (Acceptable/Poor boundary). Weakest: Consistency and Standards (1/4), Error Prevention (1/4), Aesthetic and Minimalist Design (1/4).

## CRITICAL
- C1. Recommendation-buy/sell/hold CSS classes referenced but never defined (lines 536-570, 1557) — Portfolio tab cards render unstyled.
- C2. Gradient-clip-text masthead (#2E3192→#1BFFFF) + purple metric-card gradient (#667EEA→#764BA2) — textbook AI-slop signature, first thing every viewer sees.
- C3. Custom ticker input (~line 949) has no validation; silent fallback to synthetic data with no persistent "synthetic" indicator on the chart.
- C4. Tab order buries the most differentiating content (Options Pricer, Factor Model — tabs 8-9) behind 6 more generic tabs, contradicting PRODUCT.md's stated 3-minute evaluation window.

## HIGH
- H1. No centralized color/component system: two unrelated reds (#FF4B4B vs #FF0000), custom-HTML cards on tabs 1/2/6 vs native st.metric elsewhere, ad hoc per-chart Plotly colorscales.
- H2. MACD histogram bar hardcoded `marker_color='green'` (line 623) regardless of sign — correctness bug.
- H3. Cognitive load: 6/8 checklist items fail (visual hierarchy, minimal-choices, working memory, no multi-ticker comparison, triple-repeated risk-score weighting).
- H4. Accessibility: `.sub-header` color:#666 fails contrast on #0E1117 canvas; risk-tier gradients hard to discriminate for colorblind users (medium/high both warm-toned); zero alt-text on 20+ Plotly charts.
- H5. "Dev's Risk Score"/"Dev's Proprietary Risk Assessment" framing contradicts PRODUCT.md Design Principle 4 (avoid "proprietary" language); spotlights the one unvalidated heuristic metric next to four properly-grounded methods.
- H6. Kupiec POF "❌ FAIL" badge has no plain-language gloss; risks reading as "model is broken" to non-quant viewers when it demonstrates the intended finding.

## MEDIUM
- M1. Dead `.insight-box` CSS (line 78-84), confirmed by detector, never referenced in markup, side-stripe-border anti-pattern.
- M2. Duplicate `@st.cache_data` decorator stacked on `generate_predictions` (lines 367-368).
- M3. `st.cache_data.clear()` (lines 1011, 1018) is global — wipes the 1-hour ETF factor-proxy cache on every 30s stock refresh.
- M4. Verbatim duplicated portfolio_df/display_df construction (lines 1456-1474 vs 1477-1495) — second block is dead computation.
- M5. Three `st.columns(5)` rows (lines 1117, 1327, 1765) exceed the ≤4-column guidance.
- M6. Forecast confidence band hardcoded flat ±15% (line 1284) regardless of horizon — statistically incorrect band width.

## LOW
- L1. No multi-ticker comparison view; Force Refresh button has no disabled/debounce state.
- L2. Risk-score weighting repeated 3x with no inline percentages on the bar chart axis.
- L3. Sector bar chart uses a 3-stop continuous colorscale on 2 categorical bars.
- L4. Visible source artifacts: leftover "FIXED:" comments, duplicated "FINAL RECOMMENDATION LOGIC..." comment, repeated 🎯 emoji for unrelated concepts.
